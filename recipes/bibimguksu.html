Recipe for BUSSIN’ Bibim-Guksu

Ingredients

  • Korean Wheat noodles
  • Cucumber
  • Kimchi (optional)
  • Gochujang (Korean red pepper paste)
  • Honey
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Garlic
  • Eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. Start by boiling a medium-sized pot of water for cooking the noodles
  2. While the water comes to a boil, finely chop the garlic and slice the cucumber into thin strips
  3. In a mixing bowl, add a large amount of gochujang and honey and toss the vegetables together in the sauce
  4. Once the water is boiling, add the noodles. Cook for about 3 minutes, or until soft
  5. Remove the noodles from the water and shock by running under cold water- this will stop the noodles from cooking further
  6. Mix the cooked noodles and sauce together and serve! Optional: Place a soft-boiled egg on top of the prepared noodles
