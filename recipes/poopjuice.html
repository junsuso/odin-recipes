Sussy Juice

Ingredients
  • Power greens (kale, spinach, arugula, etc)
  • Frozen blueberries
  • Flaxseed
  • Bananas
  • Pitted dates
  • Chia seeds
  • Protein powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients into a blender. Some light chopping of larger ingredients may be necessary.
  2. Blend ingredients until smooth
  3. Dump ingredients into container of choice. A mason jar is a fine receptacle.
  4. Enjoy excellent bowel movements!
