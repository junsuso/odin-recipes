Chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  • Chocolate chips
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. First, take room-temperature butter and cream together with sugar and brown sugar.
  2. Then, add flour, baking powder and baking soda to the creamed butter-and-sugar mixture.
  3. Next, add chocolate chips to the dough.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375F
  5. Roll the dough into small spheres. It is recommended to add additional chocolate chips to the tops of the spheres! More chocolate!
  6. Bake cookies for 10-12 minutes and then let cool for about 10 minutes (or as long as your patience will allow)
  7. Enjoy cookies! Yay cookies!!
